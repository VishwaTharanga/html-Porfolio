My Top 3 Movies of All - Time

Cinderella

Ambitious orphan Cinderella gets some magical help from her Fab G to realise her dreams, making a life for herself selling her signature dresses.

Dracula Untold

Vlad the Impaler, a Transylvanian prince, turns himself into a vampire to protect his family and the kingdom from invading Turkish forces.

Avengers: Endgame

This is My favourite Movie. After Thanos, an intergalactic warlord, disintegrates half of the universe, the Avengers must reunite and assemble again to reinvigorate their trounced allies and restore balance.
